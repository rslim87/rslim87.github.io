About Me

I am a full stack web developer with a preference for front end development. There is something magical about bringing designs alive with few lines of code. I strive to build websites that are functional, clean, and beautiful.

After 6+ years of working mostly as a supervisor in social service field for nonprofit organizations, I was ready for a change. I started to work toward being an EMT when I discovered CS50. I instantly fell in love with coding and building websites. After CS50, I continued to teach myself HTML, CSS, and basic JavaScript for about a year. In November 2017, I graduated from the Flatiron School 800+ hour program as a Full Stack Web Developer.

In addition to coding, I love to travel to discover new dishes and uncover rich history. You will also find me in the kitchen cooking and baking, boxing in the gym, and getting lost in bookstores.
